Main characters

Lucifer Morningstar

Lucifer is a flamboyant, arrogant, and brilliant character. He is also incredibly intelligent, cooperating with Chloe Decker in detective investigations and proving to be an invaluable companion.He is the owner of a night club called Lux, located in Los Angeles where he is also a frequent consumer of its services. Lucifer is the type of character that doesn't wanna be weighed down by serious relationships ,however over a period of seasons he is becoming mysterusly drawn to Chloe Decker, a fellow dectective.

Dan Espinoza

Dan is an L.A.P.D detective and ex-husband of Chloe Decker whom he shares a duaghter with. As Lucifer decides to help L.A.P.D solve cases,this leads Dan to interact with him. Dan dislikes Lucifer mainly because he calls him "Detective Douche" and his connection to Chloe Decker and daughter, Trixie.

Chloe Decker

Chloe Decker is an observant, proffesional and logical character. She is extremly commited to her role as a detective and likes to keep things professional, even if Lucifer her work partener decides to pull inappropriate pranks. She is an L.A.P.D detective living with her 7 year old daughter. Later in season 5 she finds out she is a gift from God and struggles to accept that.However Chloe is a very strong character emotionally and couldn't let anything stop her from loving Lucifer.

Linda Martin

Linda is a therapist living in Los Angeles and also is a main character in the show. Lucifer vists Linda for therapy on a consistent basis, usually involving personal issues with Chloe or his family. Though he never directly admits his problems, Lucifer instead supplies the Doctor with biblical metaphors and hints, requiring Linda to draw the conclusions herself.

Mazikken Smith

Mazikeen of the Lilim, usually also known by her nickname Maze, is a central character in Lucifer. She is a demon from Hell who holds the form of a young woman. For millennia, she worked under Lucifer Morningstar as one of his most trusted lieutenants and servants; he considered her his "right-hand." She is the best friend and former lover of Lucifer and formerly worked alongside him at the Lux nightclub in Los Angeles before starting a new career as a bounty hunter with the Los Angeles Police Department.

Amenadiel

Amenadiel is the eldest of all God's Angels and serves as a central character in Lucifer. Initially the primary antagonist of Season 1, he embarked on his own journey of growth and ultimately became one of Lucifer's closest allies. As the eldest in the family, Amenadiel was God's main enforcer and messenger for the majority of his lifetime, as he would be involved in fulfilling his Father's most important orders.
