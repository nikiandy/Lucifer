About Lucifer

Lucifer is a Netflix Original TV series that started out in 2016 and now has 6 seasons.
The show has a 8.1 star review on www.imdb.com and is enjoyed by millions around the world.
The plot of the show is that Lucifer the devil has decided to visit earth in human form because he got bored of hell.
However on his vacation to earth he finds his first true love, which ironically is a detective for the LAPD.

What I Enjoy About The Show

I watched Lucifer a total of 6 times through and still haven't got bored of it.
You could call it obsessive but I really enjoy many aspects of this Tv series.
I like to see Lucifer and Chloe's relationship develop as she finds out the truth.
I also like how theologically based the show is with very little fiction in it.
I really enjoy the detective work and figuring out murders with the devil himself.
I studied Christian theology so I am interested in seeing a Jewish perspective.
